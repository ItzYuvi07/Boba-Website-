Information on Boba

What is Boba?

Boba, also known as bubble tea, is a popular Taiwanese drink made from sweetened tea

mixed with milk or fruit flavors and topped with chewy tapioca pearls

Facts

Boba originated in Taiwan

Boba pearls are made from tapioca starch

The largest boba tea cup ever made was 8.2 feet tall and held over 3,000 litres of the drink
